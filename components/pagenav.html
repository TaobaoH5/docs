基础DOM结构

用于div标签， 引入pagenav.js， 设定id和c-pnav-con的类名。翻页按钮不可点时添加c-p-grey样式。具体使用方法参见JS模块 之 分页模块 

<div id="pageNavDemo" class="c-pnav-con">
    <section class="c-p-sec">
        <div class="c-p-pre">
            <span class="c-p-p"><em></em></span><a>上一页</a>
        </div>
        <div class="c-p-cur">
            <div class="c-p-arrow c-p-down">
                <span>1/3</span><span></span>
            </div>
            <select class="c-p-select">
            </select>
        </div>
        <div class="c-p-next"><a>下一页</a><span class="c-p-p"><em></em></span></div>
    </section>
</div>
